Resources

Resources for netsec.

OSCP

Study guides

[How to prepare by abatchy](http://www.abatchy.com/2017/30/how-to-prepare-for-pwkoscp-noob.html). Abatchy lists resources to learn for OSCP, segregated based on the OSCP syllabus, wargames, and vulnerable machines. [OSCP-like Vulnhub VMs](http://www.abatchy.com/2017/02/oscp-like-vulnhub-vms.html). Abatchy's list of VMs on [Vulnhub](https://www.vulnhub.com) that he believes are helpful for studying for OSCP. He lists other resources for Windows machines, as there aren't many due to licensing restrictions. [OSCP Lab and Exam Review](http://theslickgreek.com/oscp/). AJ's review of passing OSCP, including resources used. Has enumeration list / study guide toward the end.

Enumeration lists

[Basic Linux Privilege Escalation](https://blog.g0tmi1k.com/2011/08/basic-linux-privilege-escalation/). g0tmi1lk's enumeration list for Linux. [Penetration Testing Tools Cheat Sheet](https://highon.coffee/blog/penetration-testing-tools-cheat-sheet/). HighOn.Coffee's cheat sheet / enumeration for pen testing. [Windows Privilege Escalation Fundamentals](http://www.fuzzysecurity.com/tutorials/16.html). Fuzzysecurity's enumeration list for Windows. [Security](https://xapax.gitbooks.io/security/content/). Xapax's enumeration guide.

CTF / Wargames

[OverTheWire](http://overthewire.org/wargames). Variety of wargames over SSH or HTTP. [Microcorruption](https://microcorruption/login). Embedded security CTF based on MSP430 with a disassembler interface provided. [Root Me](https://www.root-me.org). Online CTF.
